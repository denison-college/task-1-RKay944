3 difficulties for the player to choose
     *
Easy – 4-5 letter words
     *
Medium – 6-7 letter words
     *
Hard – 8+ letter words
  *
Player can choose to guess either letters or the word
  *
Guessed letters are displayed
  *
Progress of the hangman is displayed
  *
Score system – the player starts with 100 points and for each incorrect guess, 10 points are removed. If the player fails to guess the word, they receive 0 points.
